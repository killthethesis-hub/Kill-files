# KILL FILE #003 --- Ondo (ONDO)

**Status:** SURVIVED --- UNDER WATCH\
**Capital Deployed:** \$0\
**Verdict:** Ondo survived the project-quality audit. ONDO still has an
incomplete token-value bridge.

## The Question

The popular thesis is simple:

> If Ondo wins the RWA market, ONDO wins.

We tested whether the second statement automatically follows from the
first.

## Adoption and Product

**Result: SURVIVED**

Ondo has material real-world product usage and infrastructure across
tokenized Treasuries, yield-bearing assets and tokenized securities.

This is not an "RWA someday" thesis.

The machine is already operating.

## Follow The Money

We separated three value buckets:

1.  Ondo the company and related commercial entities
2.  Ondo products and product holders
3.  ONDO the token

They belong to the same ecosystem. They are not automatically the same
investment thesis.

## The Missing Bridge

ONDO has established governance rights.

During this research, we could not verify a live, mandatory, recurring
route:

> Product growth → protocol revenue → ONDO market demand, buybacks,
> burns or holder cash flow

This does not mean ONDO has no value.

It means the direct economic bridge from ecosystem success to token
demand remains incomplete or unproven.

## The Fee-Switch Question

We found a governance-forum discussion proposing an automated ONDO
buyback-and-burn mechanism.

At the time of research, it remained a **Temperature Check**.

We did not verify it as a live economic mechanism.

A proposal is not execution.

Future implementation could materially change this thesis.

## Ondo Chain

Official design material discusses validators staking eligible RWAs and
other quality liquid assets selected through chain governance.

We did not verify ONDO as mandatory validator collateral.

Therefore:

> Ondo Chain success does not currently prove automatic ONDO demand.

We do not price unimplemented utility as current mandatory capture.

## Governance

ONDO has real governance architecture.

Official documentation describes governance authority over areas
including Flux parameters, the DAO treasury and ONDO emissions.

Our concern is whether governance rights currently create enough
economic demand to complete the token investment thesis.

## Dilution

ONDO has a maximum supply of 10 billion tokens.

Official allocation is heavily weighted toward ecosystem growth,
protocol development and private sales, with long-duration release
schedules.

Dilution remains a material investment variable.

Any future buyback should be compared with supply entering circulation.

> Buyback headlines are not enough. Net token economics matter.

## Strongest Bull Case

If Ondo continues scaling and ONDO gains a clear recurring capture
mechanism---or becomes economically necessary within core
infrastructure---the token thesis could improve materially.

That would force a new review.

## Strongest Bear Case

Ondo may become a major tokenized-asset platform while meaningful
economic value accrues elsewhere in the ecosystem rather than to ONDO
itself.

In that scenario:

> The project wins. The token underperforms the project narrative.

## What Would Upgrade ONDO?

1.  A live recurring revenue pipe funding transparent ONDO buybacks or
    burns
2.  Buybacks material relative to unlocks and emissions
3.  ONDO becoming economically necessary in Ondo Chain
4.  Increased economically meaningful governance activity
5.  Improved dilution-adjusted valuation asymmetry

## What Would Break The Token Thesis?

-   Product adoption continues without stronger ONDO demand
-   Token utility remains primarily governance-based
-   Dilution materially exceeds new token demand
-   Future value-capture proposals fail to become operating mechanisms
