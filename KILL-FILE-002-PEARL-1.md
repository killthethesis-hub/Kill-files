# KILL FILE #002 --- Pearl (PRL)

**Status:** THESIS KILLED FOR NOW\
**Capital Deployed:** \$0\
**Verdict:** Technology survives. Current investment thesis does not.

## The Thesis

Pearl proposes Proof-of-Useful-Work built around matrix multiplication.
The idea: AI workloads already perform matrix multiplication. If that
computation can also secure a blockchain and generate PRL rewards,
emissions may subsidize useful AI compute.

## Why It Caught Our Attention

-   Novel Proof-of-Useful-Work primitive
-   Matrix multiplication is central to AI workloads
-   Live network and active development
-   Production integration with Together AI
-   Possible compute-subsidy economic model

## Kill Layer #1 --- Is The Primitive Real?

**Result: SURVIVED**

Pearl's design allows matrix multiplication associated with AI workloads
to generate mining proofs.

But technical capability is not network-level economic enforcement.

## The Fatal Question

> Does Pearl require mining work to originate from useful AI demand?

**Current answer: NO.**

Independent empirical research demonstrated accepted mining shares using
arbitrary or random matrix inputs.

The protocol can verify matrix multiplication without proving that a
paying AI customer needed the result.

**Capability ≠ enforcement.**

## The Together AI Integration

Together AI demonstrates that the useful path can exist. Real inference
can be paired with Pearl mining so PRL emissions help offset inference
costs.

However, one integrated inference product does not prove that the wider
mining network is performing economically useful AI work.

## Token Value Capture

PRL is the native mining reward of the Pearl network.

The thesis depends on sustainable PRL demand relative to emissions and
subsidized-compute economics.

We did not find a sufficiently strong direct fee, burn, or
demand-capture loop to compensate for current uncertainty.

## Dilution And Valuation Risk

Pearl has a maximum supply of 2.1 billion PRL.

During our research, circulating market capitalization was materially
lower than fully diluted valuation.

Dilution matters.

## Miner Economics

Independent research reported negative estimated mining ROI across
tested GPU tiers at the measured PRL price.

Rapid difficulty growth also pressures miner revenue.

## Team And Disclosure

We could verify Omri Weinstein publicly as Pearl co-founder and CEO
through the Together AI partnership announcement.

We did not find sufficiently clean primary-source disclosure covering
the broader team, funding and investor structure for our standard.

This does not prove misconduct. It increases the evidence gap.

## What Survived?

-   The cryptographic research primitive
-   Ability to pair AI matrix multiplication with mining
-   Active technical development
-   Evidence that a real inference integration is possible

## What Failed?

The stronger thesis:

> Pearl network mining represents useful AI computation.

Current evidence does not support that claim at the network level.

## What Would Reopen The File?

1.  Growing percentage of network work from paid inference or training
2.  Multiple independent production integrations
3.  Transparent useful-work telemetry
4.  Sustainable miner and inference economics
5.  Stronger direct PRL demand capture
6.  Improved dilution-adjusted valuation asymmetry
